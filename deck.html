The Problem with Current Governance

Traditional voting systems—both in governments and DAOs—are fundamentally broken:

Rational Ignorance

Voters have little incentive to become informed because individual votes rarely affect outcomes. The cost of research exceeds the expected benefit.

Preference Intensity Blindness

One-token-one-vote systems treat all decisions as equally important to all voters. There's no way to signal how much you care about an issue.

Tyranny of the Majority

51% can consistently override 49%, regardless of how strongly each group feels about the issue. No compensation mechanism exists.

2 / 10

Our Solution: Tradeable Voting Rights

For each proposal, governance token holders receive tradeable voting tokens that can be bought and sold during the election period.

🎯 Market Signals

Vote prices reveal the true importance of proposals to different stakeholders

💡 Preference Weighting

Care deeply? Buy more votes. Don't care? Sell them to those who do

🎓 Expert Influence

Knowledge flows to relevant decisions without creating permanent plutocracy

💰 Natural Compensation

Losing voters can sell their votes, reducing the zero-sum nature of governance

How MarketDAO Works

1

Proposal Creation

Governance token holders submit proposals (resolutions, treasury transfers, token minting, or price changes)

2

Support Phase

Proposals need community support to trigger an election (configurable threshold)

3

Election Trigger

Voting tokens are automatically minted 1:1 for all governance token holders

4

Trading Period

Voting tokens can be freely bought and sold on any ERC1155 marketplace

5

Voting

Cast votes by sending tokens to YES/NO addresses. Early termination possible with clear majority

6

Execution

Successful proposals are automatically executed if quorum is met
