How MarketDAO Works

1

Proposal Creation

Governance token holders submit proposals (resolutions, treasury transfers, token minting, or price changes)

2

Support Phase

Proposals need community support to trigger an election (configurable threshold)

3

Election Trigger

Voting tokens are automatically minted 1:1 for all governance token holders

4

Trading Period

Voting tokens can be freely bought and sold on any ERC1155 marketplace

5

Voting

Cast votes by sending tokens to YES/NO addresses. Early termination possible with clear majority

6

Execution

Successful proposals are automatically executed if quorum is met
